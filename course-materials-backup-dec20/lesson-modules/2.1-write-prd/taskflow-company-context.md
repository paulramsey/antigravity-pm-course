# TaskFlow Company Context

## Product Overview
TaskFlow is a project management tool designed specifically for remote teams. It focuses on speed, performance, and reducing the cognitive load of managing complex projects.

## Key Stats
- **Users:** 10,000+ active users.
- **Revenue:** $2.5M ARR.
- **Team Size:** 25 people.

## Target Customers
- **Primary:** SMBs with 5-20 person teams.
- **Secondary:** Freelancers and consultants who manage multiple clients.

## Strategic Focus
1. **Speed:** Being the fastest tool in the market.
2. **Simplicity:** Avoiding the "bloat" of enterprise tools like Jira or Asana.
3. **Collaboration:** Deep integration with tools like Slack and Zoom.

## Current Roadmap Priorities
- Improving onboarding (Template Library).
- Reducing notification noise (Triage/Digests).
- Expanding interaction modes (Voice, Mobile).
