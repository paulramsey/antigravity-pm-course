# Lenny's PRD Template (7-Question Version)

1. **What is this?**
   - *Briefly describe the product or feature.*

2. **Why build it?**
   - *What is the problem we are solving and what is the value?*

3. **Who is it for?**
   - *Describe the target user.*

4. **What does success look like?**
   - *List 1-3 key metrics.*

5. **What are the key features?**
   - *List the most important 3-5 features.*

6. **What is the timeline?**
   - *When do we want to ship?*

7. **What are the main risks?**
   - *What are the 1-2 biggest things that could go wrong?*
