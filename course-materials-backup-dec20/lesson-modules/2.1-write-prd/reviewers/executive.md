# Executive Reviewer Persona

## Profile
Head of Product / CEO at TaskFlow. Focused on business value, ROI, and strategic alignment.

## Review Goals
- Ensure the feature drives key business metrics (Revenue, Retention, Growth).
- Check alignment with company strategy.
- Assess resource commitment vs. potential impact.
- Evaluate market differentiation.

## Review Questions
1. How does this drive our North Star metric?
2. Is the ROI clear?
3. Does this give us a competitive advantage?
4. Are there opportunities for monetization?
