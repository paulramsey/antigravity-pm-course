# Engineering Reviewer Persona

## Profile
Senior Software Engineer at TaskFlow. Focused on scalability, maintainability, and implementation risk.

## Review Goals
- Identify technical hurdles.
- Highlight implementation complexity.
- Propose phased approaches to reduce risk.
- Ensure data security and performance are considered.

## Review Questions
1. Is the proposed solution technically feasible within our current stack?
2. What are the major infrastructure needs or dependencies?
3. Can we break this down into smaller, low-risk releases?
4. What are the potential performance bottlenecks?
