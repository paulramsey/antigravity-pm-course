# Experiment Readout: Guided Onboarding with Sample Project

**Date:** December 2024
**Experiment:** A/B Test (50/50 Split)
**Sample Size:** 8,000 Users (4k Control, 4k Treatment)
**Status:** Conclusion Reached

## Executive Summary
**Recommendation: SHIP to Small Teams (5-20), REVERT for Enterprise (100+)**

The Guided Onboarding experiment showed a modest +6.4pp lift overall, but this masked a massive success for our core segment. Small teams saw a **+11.4pp lift in activation** and a **3x increase in team invites**, while enterprise users saw a negative impact. The feature drives significantly higher user quality, with activated users completing **2.6x more tasks** in their first week.

## 5. Recommendation & Next Steps

1.  **Launch Immediate:** Roll out Guided Onboarding to 100% of **Small Teams (5-29 users)**.
2.  **Product Logic:** Disable Guided Onboarding for **Enterprise (100+)** signups (revert to old flow).
3.  **Investigate:** Launch a follow-up qualitative study with Mid-Market/Enterprise users to design a tailored "Admin Setup" flow for them.
