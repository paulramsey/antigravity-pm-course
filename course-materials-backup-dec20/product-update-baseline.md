# Product Update: Workspace Sharing is Here!

We are excited to announce the launch of our newest feature: **Workspace Sharing**.

Collaboration is at the heart of everything we do, and with this update, we're making it easier than ever to work together on your projects. Whether you're working with a small team or a large department, Workspace Sharing gives you the tools you need to stay organized and productive.

### What's New?

*   **Invite Team Members:** Easily add colleagues to your workspace or project using their email address.
*   **Permissions Management:** Control who can view, edit, or manage your projects with granular permission settings.
*   **Real-time Collaboration:** See updates from your team as they happen, ensuring everyone is on the same page.
*   **Centralized Communication:** Keep all your project-related discussions in one place.

### Why We Built This

We know that managing complex projects requires seamless coordination. Our new sharing capabilities are designed to reduce friction and help your team achieve more, faster. By allowing you to share entire workspaces or individual projects, we're giving you the flexibility to structure your work exactly how you want.

### How to Get Started

Simply navigate to your project settings or the workspace dashboard and look for the new "Share" button. From there, you can invite your team and start collaborating immediately.

We can't wait to see how you use Workspace Sharing to take your productivity to the next level!

Questions? Feedback? Reach out to our support team at any time.
